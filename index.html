About Me

As a musician, I am a member of one of Broadway's most popular musicals. I have also performed regularly with some of the nation's top orchestras and symphonies. The hard work, attention to detail and dedication it took me to achieve these dreams, are just a few of the qualities I will bring to any project I work on as a web developer. Hi! My name is Jessica Wiersma and I want to help you with your web development needs!

I enjoy working in fields where your work is never quite done - where there is always something else to perfect, a new skill to learn, an old skill to elevate. Web Development offers me just that - a chance to continually learn and hone skills. The ability to work independently within a team setting to achieve a collective goal is another aspect of web development I thoroughly enjoy.

My experience managing teams, being a team member and working with diverse groups of people coupled with my self-discipline, attention to detail and determination to produce the best possible product, make me an invaluable member to any project.
